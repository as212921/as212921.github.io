Peering

We operate an open peering policy. Peering is welcomed either at one of the exchanges which we have a presence, or by tunneling to one of our routing servers (either London, UK or Frankfurt, Germany). Our PeeringDB entry is available here. Our AS-set is AS-AYING.

Requirements

  • Should have an up-to-date PeeringDB entry
  • Should have a NOC contact email which is monitored regularly and issues responded to
  • Must only send us traffic destined to networks that we announced
  • Must not use us as the default route
